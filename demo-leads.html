Dashboard Features

Everything you need to manage leads — nothing you don't.

⚡

Real-Time Updates

Lead records appear the moment a call ends or a form is submitted. Supabase Realtime subscription means zero refresh needed.

🔴

Urgency Scoring

Every record is scored by urgency — Urgent, High, Normal, Low. AI flags emergency language automatically. Urgent leads surface first.

🗂️

Source Tracking

Know exactly where every lead came from — AI Voice call, web form intake, missed-call text-back, or email. Full source attribution.

✅

Mark & Resolve

One-click to mark a lead as contacted, resolved, or follow-up needed. Status updates sync across the dashboard instantly.

🔍

Filter & Search

Filter by urgency, source, status, or date. Full-text search across lead records. Find any record in seconds.

📊

Volume Analytics

Daily totals, resolution rates, urgency breakdown, and source mix — all visible at a glance on the overview panel.
