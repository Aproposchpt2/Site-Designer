01
Intelligent Conversational Agent

An always-on front desk that actually listens.

Most businesses lose the lead the moment a call goes unanswered or dumps into a menu. This agent picks up every call, speaks naturally, and turns the conversation itself into qualified pipeline — day or night, in English or Spanish.

Caller recognition by Caller ID
Returning callers are identified the instant they ring and greeted by name — "Welcome back, Jeffrey — how can I help you today?" The relationship picks up right where it left off.
Full conversation memory per caller
Every prior interaction is remembered, so a caller never has to repeat themselves and the agent references past context naturally, like a rep who actually knows them.
Intelligent call routing by intent
The agent understands why someone is calling and routes them to the right person, queue, or workflow — not a rigid "press 4 for billing" tree.
Natural language understanding
No "press 1." Callers simply talk the way they would to a human being, and the agent understands intent, nuance, and follow-up questions in real time.
Appointment & callback scheduling
Books appointments and callbacks live during the call and writes them straight to the calendar — the conversation ends with a confirmed slot, not a promise to call back.
Lead qualification during the call
Asks the right qualifying questions inside the conversation, so leads land in your pipeline already scored and sorted — not as raw, unworked names.
Multilingual — English + Spanish
Serves both markets natively in the caller's own language, with no separate line and no bilingual staff to hire. You capture leads you were quietly losing before.
24/7 availability — never misses a call
Answers at 2 p.m. and 2 a.m. with identical quality. After-hours and overflow calls become booked appointments instead of lost voicemails the competition picks up.
