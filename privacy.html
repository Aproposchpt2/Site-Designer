Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

FlowDesk Pro is operated by Apropos Group LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through FlowDesk Pro intake forms, contact workflows, and related customer support communications.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you submit an intake request or contact us:

  • Name, email address, phone number, business name, and industry.
  • Information about your service request, project needs, appointment request, or support question.
  • Your preferred contact method and preferred callback time.
  • SMS consent status when you choose to opt in to text message communication.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to requests, coordinate appointments or callbacks, provide support, prepare service follow-up, and improve FlowDesk Pro intake and automation workflows.

SMS Privacy and Non-Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared, sold, rented, or transferred to third parties for marketing purposes. Mobile numbers may be used only to send messages related to the request, appointment, service coordination, support, or follow-up that the user opted in to receive.

Service Providers

We may use trusted service providers to operate our website, database, email notifications, SMS delivery, hosting, and related business systems. These providers are used only to deliver and support FlowDesk Pro services and are not permitted to use your information for their own marketing.

Your Choices

SMS consent is optional. You may choose not to opt in to SMS and may still submit an intake request. If you opt in to SMS, you may opt out at any time by replying STOP. You may reply HELP for help.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards to protect submitted information. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Apropos Group LLC through the FlowDesk Pro intake form or by using the contact information provided on the FlowDesk Pro website.
